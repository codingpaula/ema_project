What is a Telegram Bot?

A Telegram Bot is an artificial User in the messaging app Telegram. The EMA Bot, that is connected to this web application, can remind you, when your tasks are due, or tell you about your tasks, when you are on the road.

How do I use this EMA Bot?

Steps to configure the Bot:
  1. Click on the link below and get introduced to the EMA Bot!
  2. If you don't have the Telegram App yet, download and install it, as you are told there! (There is also a Desktop Version, if you want to try out things first.)
  3. Follow the instructions given to you by the EMA Bot.
